Why Play Browser Games?

Browser games offer something no other gaming platform can match: zero barrier to play. No console required. No download. No account creation. No subscription fee. Just click a link and you're playing within seconds — on any device, from any location in the world.

This immediacy makes browser games perfect for every kind of gamer. Students play during lunch breaks on school Chromebooks. Office workers unwind for five minutes between meetings. Casual players discover a new favourite without the commitment of purchasing anything. Hardcore gamers challenge leaderboards on IO titles that have genuine skill ceilings.

Modern browser gaming has also come a long way technically. Thanks to WebGL, WebAssembly, and fast CDNs, today's browser games feature high-fidelity 3D graphics, real-time multiplayer with hundreds of simultaneous players, and physics engines that match standalone apps. The gap between a "browser game" and a "real game" has effectively disappeared. What remains is pure convenience — and the Poki2 network is built to deliver that convenience across eight specialized destinations covering every genre.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about playing free browser games on the Poki2 network.

Are all games on Poki2 completely free? +

Yes — every game across the entire Poki2 network is free to play with no purchase required. The sites are supported by display advertising (powered by Google AdSense), which keeps everything free for players. There are no premium tiers, no paywalled games, no in-app purchases you need to make progress, and no subscription fees. You can play every game on every site as many times as you want at no cost.

Do I need to create an account to play? +

No account is required to play any game on the Poki2 network. You can go directly from clicking a link to playing a game in seconds — no registration form, no email verification, no password to remember. Some games save progress locally in your browser using local storage, which means your progress persists between sessions without any account. If you clear your browser data, local game saves may be lost, but no account sign-in is ever required.

Do the games work on mobile and tablet? +

Most games on the Poki2 network are designed to work on both desktop and mobile browsers. Touch controls are supported on games that have been optimised for touchscreen. However, some games — particularly those that require keyboard shortcuts, precise mouse control, or split-keyboard two-player input — are better suited to desktop play. Each game page notes the recommended platform. Mobile users on iOS Safari and Android Chrome will find the largest selection of compatible titles at Poki2 Play.

Do the games work on school Chromebooks or restricted networks? +

Many games on the Poki2 network are specifically chosen to work in school and office environments. Unblocked Games G+ is our dedicated school-safe portal with 474+ games that work on most filtered networks and Chromebooks. All Poki2 sites are served over HTTPS from Cloudflare's global CDN, which gives them a better chance of being accessible on filtered networks than older gaming portals. Whether a specific site is accessible depends on your school's content filter configuration — we cannot guarantee access on every network, but Unblocked G+ is designed with this use case as the primary priority.

How do I suggest a game for the network? +

We welcome game suggestions from the community. Use the Poki2 contact form and include the game's name, the platform or URL where it is hosted, and note which Poki2 site you think it fits best. Our editorial team reviews every suggestion. We evaluate new candidates based on control responsiveness, performance on modest hardware, content appropriateness, and how it fits the existing catalogue. We add new games monthly and prioritise titles with strong community requests.

Why are some games loading slowly? +

Game load speed depends on your connection speed, browser, and the size of the game's assets. On your first visit to a game, all assets must be downloaded — this can take a few seconds on a slow connection. On repeat visits, cached assets load nearly instantly. If a game is consistently slow, try: (1) closing other browser tabs to free up memory, (2) switching to a wired internet connection if on Wi-Fi, (3) ensuring your browser is up to date, or (4) disabling browser extensions that intercept network requests. All Poki2 sites are served from Cloudflare's global CDN, which minimises distance-based latency.

Are the games safe for children? +

All games in the Poki2 network are manually reviewed before listing. We do not host games with adult content, graphic violence, or gambling mechanics. Most titles are appropriate for players 10 and up. For younger children (ages 4–12), we are building a dedicated Kids Games portal with age-appropriate content and a no-ads-during-play policy — coming soon. In the meantime, the puzzle, casual, and platformer categories on Poki2 Play have the highest concentration of family-friendly titles.
