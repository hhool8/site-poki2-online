About Poki2

Last updated: April 2026

Our Mission

Poki2 exists to make high-quality browser games accessible to everyone, everywhere — at school, at the office, or relaxing at home. We believe that great online games should be free to play, impossible to miss because of geographic restrictions, and zero-effort to start: no download, no account, no waiting.

Since launching in 2023, Poki2 has grown from a single game portal into a network of specialized sites covering everything from classic arcade action to school-safe unblocked titles. We maintain every site in the network ourselves, hand-curating each game library to keep quality high and load times fast.

The Poki2 Network

Today the Poki2 network consists of three live sites and five in active development:

  • Poki2 Play (play.poki2.online) — Our flagship game portal with 200+ games across action, puzzle, racing, sports, and adventure categories. Launching soon.
  • AZ Games (azgames.poki2.online) — 300+ games organized from A to Z, including IO multiplayer, clicker, and 2-player titles.
  • Unblocked G+ (unblocked-games-g-plus.poki2.online) — School-safe unblocked games that work on restricted networks and Chromebooks.

Additional portals focusing on kids' games, racing, puzzle, IO multiplayer, and stickman action are currently under development and will launch throughout 2026.

How We Select Games

Every game in the Poki2 network goes through a manual review before it appears on any of our sites. We check that:

  • The game loads in under 5 seconds on a standard broadband connection.
  • Controls are responsive and work on both keyboard/mouse and touchscreen.
  • The content is appropriate for the target audience of the specific site.
  • The game does not contain intrusive pop-ups, deceptive UI, or misleading mechanics.
  • The game iframe is either self-hosted or sourced from a trusted partner under appropriate licensing.

Our Team

Poki2 is run by a small independent team of game enthusiasts and web developers based in the United States. We do not have a large corporate structure — which means faster decisions, a genuine passion for the games we feature, and a direct line of communication with our players. If you spot a broken game, a bug, or simply want to say hello, we read every message sent to our contact form.

Advertising & Revenue

All Poki2 sites are free to use and supported by display advertising through Google AdSense (publisher ID: ca-pub-6199549323873133). Ads may appear on page margins, between content sections, and in other standard placements. We do not show ads inside active game iframes, and we do not use pop-up or pop-under advertising.

Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by using the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool. For full details see our Privacy Policy.

We may also earn referral revenue if you click on certain partner links. These are clearly labelled and do not affect our editorial independence — we never recommend a game because we are paid to do so.

Content Ownership & DMCA

Game content hosted on our servers was either created by us, licensed from partners, or embedded with the permission of the original developer. If you believe content on any Poki2 site infringes your copyright, please review our DMCA Policy and submit a removal request to dmca@poki2.online. We aim to respond within 72 hours.
