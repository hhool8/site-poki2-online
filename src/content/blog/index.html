Browser Games Blog

Guides, recommendations, and insights from the Poki2 team — helping you discover the best free games on the web.

About This Blog

The Poki2 Blog is where our editorial team shares in-depth guides, curated game recommendations, and practical advice for browser gaming enthusiasts. Whether you’re new to browser gaming and looking for somewhere to start, a student hunting for games that work through a school firewall, or a veteran IO player looking for your next obsession — you’ve found the right place.

Every article is written by the Poki2 team based on hands-on play experience. We test every game we recommend, and we update our articles whenever the gaming landscape changes. You won’t find AI-generated filler here — just honest, practical writing from people who genuinely love browser gaming.

What We Cover

Our articles fall into four main categories:

  • Top Lists & Recommendations: Curated picks for specific audiences, moods, or genres. If you don’t know what to play next, start here. Our selection covers everything from fast-paced reflex games to slow-burn idle titles you can run in the background all day.
  • Genre Guides: Deep dives into specific game types — IO games, unblocked games, multiplayer titles, and more. These articles explain what makes a genre tick, give examples of the best games in it, and help you decide whether it’s right for you before you invest time playing.
  • How-To & Practical Guides: Solving real problems players encounter — how to access games at school, how to improve your performance in competitive titles, how to find safe sites with quality content, and how to get the most out of your browser’s gaming capabilities.
  • Editor’s Picks & Reviews: Individual game spotlights where we go deeper than a thumbnail and a tag line. Where is it available? How does it play on mobile vs desktop? What’s the skill ceiling? Who will love it and who won’t? These reviews answer the questions you actually have before committing to a new game.

Games We Write About

Our coverage draws primarily from the Poki2 network: AZ Games (300+ titles spanning IO, clicker, adventure, and sports categories), Unblocked Games G+ (school-safe classics that work on restricted networks), and the broader browser gaming ecosystem. Where a game we recommend is available across multiple portals, we’ll tell you which one offers the best version.

We don’t accept payment to recommend games, and we don’t include titles we haven’t actually played. All game descriptions are based on direct experience with the current live version of each game, not marketing copy from developers.

How Often We Publish

We publish new articles roughly twice per month. Our most comprehensive guides, like the complete IO games explainer and the full browser game top-15 list, take several weeks of research and play-testing to produce. Shorter recommendation pieces come out more frequently. Bookmark this page or check back monthly to see what’s new.
