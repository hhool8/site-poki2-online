What Is Moto X3M?

Moto X3M is a stunt motorbike game developed by Madpuffers and available free in your browser. You race through obstacle-filled courses on a dirt bike, performing backflips and front flips mid-air to reduce your finish time. The level design escalates from simple ramps to elaborate clockwork death traps, and the star-rating system gives completionists a reason to replay every stage until they nail the perfect run.

How to Play Moto X3M

Reach the finish flag on each level as quickly as possible without crashing. Your rider can respawn from checkpoints, but each crash costs precious seconds on your time. Mid-air rotations (holding back to backflip, forward to front flip) reduce your time on landing — the more rotations you complete cleanly, the better your star rating. Nailing multiple full rotations before landing is the fastest route to three stars on every level.

Controls

  • Up Arrow / W — accelerate
  • Down Arrow / S — brake / reverse
  • Left Arrow / A — lean back / rotate backward (backflip)
  • Right Arrow / D — lean forward / rotate forward (front flip)

Tips for High Scores

Rotate on every airtime. Even tiny ramps give enough air for a partial flip. Completing even a quarter-rotation on landing shaves time. Train yourself to automatically tap back or forward whenever the wheels leave the ground.

Be aggressive on inclines. Holding accelerator at full power up slopes maintains the momentum you need to launch high enough for full rotations.

Use rear brake on drops. When descending a steep slope, tapping the rear brake (down arrow) tilts the bike backward and lets the front wheel land softly — avoiding crashes from nose-diving.

Memorize checkpoints. Learn where checkpoints are on each level. A deliberate crash just after a checkpoint with a clean path to the finish is often faster than a cautious approach that wastes time on a difficult obstacle.

The Moto X3M Series

Moto X3M has spawned multiple sequels: Winter Pool Party, Spooky Land, and Bike Beach. Each installment introduces a new theme and fresh obstacle types while keeping the same satisfying core loop. Starting with the original Moto X3M and working through the series is one of the best ways to spend free time in a browser — no installation, no account, just bikes and ramps.
