What Is Animal Craft?

Animal Craft is a fusion survival game — combine two different animals to create a bizarre hybrid, then explore the world, battle enemies, and survive. Each new hybrid has unique abilities and stat profiles.

How to Play

Select two animals from your inventory and fuse them at the crafting table. Your hybrid spawns into the world ready to fight. Explore biomes, collect resources to fund more fusions, and defeat increasingly tough enemies to unlock rarer animal types.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys — move
  • Left Click — attack
  • E — interact / craft

Tips

Experiment freely — unusual combinations often produce hybrid stats that counter specific enemy types. Prioritise mobility fusions early so you can escape danger while learning the map.
