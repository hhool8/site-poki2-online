What Is Blocky Rider?

Blocky Rider is a block-art motorcycle game set in city streets packed with traffic. Lane-split through vehicles, avoid crashes, earn coins, and unlock new bikes. Multiple modes include endless, missions, and time attack.

How to Play

Tilt your phone (mobile) or use arrow keys (desktop) to steer between lanes. Speed increases automatically — your job is to dodge every car and bus. Near-misses award bonus coins. Use coins to unlock faster, more manoeuvrable bikes.

Controls

  • ← / → — change lanes
  • ↑ / ↓ — speed up / slow down

Tips

Look two or three cars ahead, not at the car immediately in front of you. The higher-tier bikes steer faster — unlock the second bike as soon as possible because its reaction time dramatically reduces crash rate.
