What Is Bat Smash?

Bat Smash is a casual batting distance game. Time your swing to connect with the baseball and send it as far as possible. Earn coins based on distance and invest in better bats, power-ups, and launch angle modifiers.

How to Play

A ball is pitched toward you automatically. Click or tap to swing your bat — the timing determines both power and angle. A perfect hit at the sweet spot sends the ball flying the maximum distance. Collect mid-air coins on the ball's trajectory.

Controls

  • Click / Tap — swing bat

Tips

Aim for the yellow zone on the timing indicator — it gives the best combination of power and angle. Use a portion of each run's earnings on the bat upgrade first; it has the highest distance return per coin spent.
