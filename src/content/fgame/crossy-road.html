What Is Crossy Road?

Crossy Road is an endless arcade hopper inspired by the classic Frogger concept but built with modern 3D voxel graphics and a huge cast of collectible characters. Originally developed by Hipster Whale for mobile, it plays beautifully as a browser game. Your character hops through an infinite world of roads, rivers, rail tracks, and lily pads — one hop at a time — while avoiding vehicles, trains, and the eagle that sweeps you away if you stand still too long.

How to Play Crossy Road

Tap or press the forward key to hop one square ahead. Press left or right to move sideways, and press back to retreat. The world scrolls forward as you progress; falling behind the screen boundary ends the run instantly. Roads have cars moving at different speeds; rivers have logs and lily pads — hop only to solid platforms. As your distance increases, traffic speeds up and gaps between safe platforms narrow.

Controls

  • Up Arrow / W — hop forward
  • Left Arrow / A — hop left
  • Right Arrow / D — hop right
  • Down Arrow / S — hop backward
  • Click / Tap — hop in direction of click (on supported versions)

Tips for Going Further

Never stand still. The eagle targets you after roughly three seconds of inactivity. Always be moving, even if only one hop sideways.

Hug the right lane on roads. Traffic generally moves left-to-right and right-to-left in alternating lanes. Positioning on the right side of a waiting gap usually gives you a running start for the next available opening.

Plan three hops ahead on rivers. Logs move at different speeds. Before jumping to a log, check where it will be in two hops — a log moving right means you'll drift with it, potentially into a wall.

Sacrifice one lateral hop to gain two forward. When roads are heavily congested, moving sideways to a thinner traffic section often creates a better crossing opportunity than waiting in a dangerous lane.

Characters and Unlocks

Crossy Road includes a large roster of collectible characters — chickens, aliens, athletes, mash-up celebrities — each with its own visual world theme. Coins collected during runs are spent in the prize machine to unlock random characters. Some characters reveal hidden secret worlds with completely different environments and obstacles, rewarding players who collect widely.

FAQ

Q: What causes the eagle to appear?
A: The eagle appears if you remain idle for several seconds; keep moving to avoid it.
Q: Are characters only cosmetic?
A: Characters are mostly cosmetic; some have minor gameplay differences in specific ports, but generally they don't grant unfair advantages.
