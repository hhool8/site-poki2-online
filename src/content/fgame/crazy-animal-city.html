What Is Crazy Animal City?

Crazy Animal City is an open-world 3D animal simulator — choose from several animals, roam a fully explorable city, and cause mayhem. The sandbox design has no win condition; it rewards exploration and creative destruction.

How to Play

Select your animal, then spawn into the city. Walk, run, and interact with objects and NPCs freely. Different animals have unique abilities — some can climb buildings, others charge through vehicles. Unlock new animals and city zones by completing optional objectives.

Controls

  • WASD — move
  • Mouse — look around
  • Space — jump / special action
  • Left Click — attack / interact

Tips

Try every animal at least once — their movement speeds and attack styles make each feel like a different game. The city has hidden collectibles that unlock bonus animals; explore rooftops and alleyways to find them all.
