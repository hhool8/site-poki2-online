What Is Astro Tycoon?

Astro Tycoon is a space idle tycoon where you mine minerals from distant planets and reinvest profits to reach farther and farther into the galaxy. Each new planet unlocks rarer minerals and bigger multipliers.

How to Play

Send mining ships to nearby planets to collect minerals automatically. Sell minerals for credits and reinvest in faster ships, larger cargo holds, and new mining tech. Unlock further planets by meeting credit milestones.

Controls

  • Mouse / Click — manage ships and upgrades

Tips

Balance spending between ship speed and cargo capacity — a fast ship with tiny cargo earns less than a slow ship with maximum hold. Always buy the next-tier planet access when you can; new planets multiply income far more than upgrading existing routes.
