What Is 2048 Drop?

2048 Drop is a falling-chip merge puzzle. Chips drop from above — place matching numbers side by side and they combine into the next value. Your goal is to build a tile worth 2048 before the board fills up.

How to Play

Position the falling chip with ← → keys or your mouse, then let it drop. Identical numbers touching each other merge instantly. Plan every drop — one misplaced tile can block all future combos.

Controls

  • ← / → — move chip left or right
  • ↓ — drop faster
  • Mouse — click column to drop

Tips

Keep your largest tile in a corner and build toward it. Clear chain-merges whenever possible to keep the board clear. If you get stuck, look for the column that unblocks the most future moves.
