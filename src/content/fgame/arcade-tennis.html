What Is Arcade Tennis?

Arcade Tennis is a fast-paced 2D tennis game where you control a quirky player on a compact court. Time your swings, direct your shots with angle, and outlast your opponent in rally-based matches.

How to Play

Move your player with A/D or arrow keys. Press the attack button when the ball is in range to swing. The angle of your swing determines shot direction — aim cross-court to keep your opponent off balance. Win games to progress through the tournament bracket.

Controls

  • A / D or ← → — move player
  • Spacebar or Z — swing

Tips

Watch the ball arc, not your player — react earlier. Hitting near the edges of your racket generates angled shots that are harder to reach. Mix deep baseline shots with drop shots to move your opponent around the court.
