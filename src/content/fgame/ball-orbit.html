What Is Ball Orbit?

Ball Orbit is a baseball-style distance game — swing at the perfect moment to launch your ball as far as possible, earn coins from the distance, and spend them on upgrades that make the ball travel further each hit.

How to Play

Click or tap when the power meter is at its peak to swing. A full-power hit at the optimal angle launches the ball on a long arc. Use earned coins to upgrade bat power, ball weight, and landing bonuses. Each round gives you one swing.

Controls

  • Click / Tap — swing bat

Tips

Hit when the meter fills completely — a slightly late hit loses significant distance. Prioritise upgrading the base power stat first; it scales better than the angle or bounce bonuses in early runs.
