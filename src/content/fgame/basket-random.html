What Is Basket Random?

Basket Random is a chaotic 2-player basketball game where the court, players, and ball physics randomise every round. Both players control stretched, ragdoll-style characters — whoever scores more points in the time limit wins.

How to Play

Each player has a single button — pressing it makes your character jump and swing toward the ball. Timing is everything because the physics change between rounds. Steal opportunities from your opponent and aim for the basket on your side.

Controls

  • Player 1: W — jump / action
  • Player 2: ↑ — jump / action
  • Single player: Click

Tips

Since physics change every round, adapt quickly rather than relying on a fixed strategy. Watch where the ball spawns each round — being closest to the spawn point is a huge early advantage.
