What Is Chicken Jockey Combat?

Chicken Jockey Combat is a first-person zombie survival shooter themed around the Minecraft Chicken Jockey meme. Defend your base from escalating zombie waves using a range of weapons, managing ammo and positioning under pressure.

How to Play

Move with WASD, aim with the mouse, and shoot with left click. Zombies approach from all sides — prioritise the closest threats and aim for the head. Between waves, spend earned credits on new weapons, ammunition upgrades, and defensive structures.

Controls

  • WASD — move
  • Mouse — aim
  • Left Click — fire
  • 1–3 — switch weapons

Tips

Stand in the centre of your base during early waves so no direction is over-exposed. Upgrade your primary weapon's damage before buying new weapon types — a high-damage primary clears waves faster than a varied but weak arsenal.
