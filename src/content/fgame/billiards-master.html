What Is Billiards Master?

Billiards Master is a realistic pool simulation with multiple game modes — 8-ball, 9-ball, and practice. It features accurate ball physics, spin control, and a full shot-power system for both casual and competitive play.

How to Play

Aim the cue by moving your mouse around the cue ball. Click and drag backward to set power, then release to shoot. Apply spin by clicking the spin indicator on the cue ball graphic — top-spin, back-spin, and side-spin all change how the cue ball reacts after contact.

Controls

  • Mouse Aim — direction of shot
  • Click + Drag — set shot power
  • Spin Pad — apply English / spin

Tips

Always plan two shots ahead — potting a ball counts for nothing if the cue ball ends up snookered. Back-spin is the most useful technique early on; it stops the cue ball dead after contact, giving you precise position control.
