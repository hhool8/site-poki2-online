What Is Bike Xtreme?

Bike Xtreme is an extreme motocross racing game with steep hills, ramps, and trick-based scoring. Complete race courses while pulling off flips and jumps for bonus points — crash too hard and you restart the checkpoint.

How to Play

Accelerate with the gas button (or right arrow), lean forward and backward mid-air with the tilt keys to land cleanly. Hitting a ramp with full speed launches you into flip territory — hold the lean key to rotate and release to land upright.

Controls

  • ← → — accelerate / brake
  • ↑ / ↓ — lean forward / backward

Tips

Land flat or slightly front-heavy to maintain speed after jumps. Rear-heavy landings bounce you off the track. Time your flips conservatively early on — an incomplete rotation means a crash and a checkpoint restart.
