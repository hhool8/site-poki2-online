What Is BitLife?

BitLife is a text-based life simulation game. You are born as a random character anywhere in the world and make decisions from childhood through old age — education, relationships, careers, crimes, and more. Every playthrough generates a unique life story.

How to Play

Each year of your character's life, BitLife presents a series of events and choices. Tap or click the option you want. Your stats — happiness, health, smarts, and looks — evolve based on your choices. Complete challenges, achieve life goals, and discover the many hidden paths and career options.

Controls

  • Click / Tap — make choices

Tips

Study hard in school to unlock high-paying careers. Keep health high by going to the gym and eating well. Try to complete the special challenge ribbons for hidden achievements — they require very specific life paths.
