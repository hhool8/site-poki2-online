What Is Slither.io?

Slither.io is a massively multiplayer snake game where you grow by eating glowing orbs and outmaneuver other players to survive.

How to Play

Collect orbs to grow longer. Use boosts to escape or trap other snakes, but boosting shortens your length slightly.

Controls

  • Mouse / Touch — move your snake; click to boost
