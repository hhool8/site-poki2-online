What Is Bricky Break?

Bricky Break is an arcade brick-breaker — launch a ball to destroy colorful brick formations and collect bonus balls for chain reactions. Each level has a different layout with special bricks that take multiple hits or drop power-ups.

How to Play

Aim your launcher by moving the mouse and click to shoot. The ball ricochets off walls and hits bricks. Collect extra balls on the field to multiply your projectiles. Clear all bricks to complete the level. Some bricks require multiple hits — they are darker in colour.

Controls

  • Mouse — aim direction
  • Left Click — launch balls

Tips

Aim at clusters of bricks in the centre of the field so the ball ricochets multiple times per shot. Always pick up bonus balls — a large multiball volley clears entire columns faster than targeting individual bricks.
