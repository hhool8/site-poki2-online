What Is Astro Robot Clicker?

Astro Robot Clicker is an idle clicker set in space — click your robot to generate energy, then invest in upgrades that automate production and send your robot on missions to distant planets. The further you explore, the higher the energy multipliers.

How to Play

Click the central robot to earn energy points. Spend points on upgrades in the right panel — passive generators, click multipliers, and planetary missions. Once you have enough automation, the game runs itself and you can focus on deciding which upgrades to prioritise.

Controls

  • Left Click — click robot to earn energy
  • Mouse — navigate upgrade menus

Tips

Invest in passive generators as soon as possible — they compound over time and outpace manual clicking quickly. Complete planetary missions for large one-time energy bonuses that can fund several upgrade tiers at once.
