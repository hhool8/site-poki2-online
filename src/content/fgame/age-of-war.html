What Is Age of War?

Age of War is a real-time strategy game where you evolve a civilization from the Stone Age through the present, defending your base while attacking the enemy's. Deploy units, manage resources, and unlock increasingly powerful technologies across five ages.

How to Play

Click the unit buttons on the left to spawn fighters. They automatically march toward the enemy base. Earn XP by killing enemies — fill the bar to evolve to the next age and unlock stronger units and turrets. Click turrets on your base to fire them manually for extra damage.

Controls

  • Left Click — spawn units, fire turrets
  • Unit Buttons — select which unit to deploy

Tips

Evolve to the next age as fast as possible — higher-age units counter lower-age enemies easily. Spend gold on the best single unit type rather than spreading resources; overwhelming one strong unit beats mixed cheap units.
