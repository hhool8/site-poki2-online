What Is Chicken Jockey Clicker?

Chicken Jockey Clicker is an idle clicker starring the iconic Minecraft Chicken Jockey — a baby zombie riding a chicken. Click to earn points, unlock upgrade tiers, and collect increasingly bizarre Chicken Jockey character skins as your score climbs.

How to Play

Click the Chicken Jockey character in the centre to earn coins. Spend coins in the upgrade shop: click multipliers boost each tap, passive generators earn coins automatically, and prestige upgrades multiply your entire income. The deeper upgrade tiers unlock new character designs.

Controls

  • Left Click — click Chicken Jockey
  • Mouse — buy upgrades

Tips

The upgrade pacing is well-balanced — passive generators become available quickly and are worth buying immediately. Check the milestone panel regularly; some passive multiplier unlocks are hidden behind total-click counts rather than coin thresholds.
