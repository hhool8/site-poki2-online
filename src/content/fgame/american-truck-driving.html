What Is American Truck Driving?

American Truck Driving is a 3D driving simulation. Take the wheel of a heavy-duty truck across American highways, navigate traffic and weather, and deliver cargo on time across long-haul routes.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to steer and accelerate. Follow the GPS route on the minimap to reach your destination. Stay in your lane, observe speed limits on tight turns, and brake early for sharp corners — trucks take longer to stop than cars.

Controls

  • W / ↑ — accelerate
  • S / ↓ — brake / reverse
  • A / D or ← → — steer

Tips

Slow down before turns — a loaded truck has serious momentum. Use the minimap constantly to anticipate junctions early. Completing deliveries without damage earns bonus cash for upgrades.
