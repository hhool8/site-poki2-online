What Is Blocky Hunter?

Blocky Hunter is a first-person zombie survival shooter with blocky, Minecraft-inspired visuals. Fight through waves of undead enemies including special boss zombies, manage your ammo, and survive as long as possible.

How to Play

Use WASD to move and the mouse to aim and shoot. Headshots deal critical damage and conserve ammo significantly. Survive each wave to earn upgrade currency — spend it on new weapons, increased fire rate, or larger ammo capacity between waves.

Controls

  • WASD — move
  • Mouse — aim
  • Left Click — shoot
  • R — reload

Tips

Always prioritise headshots — they deal double damage and stretch your ammo through many more waves. Kite enemies in circles rather than standing still; a moving target is much harder to hit in waves with ranged zombie types.
