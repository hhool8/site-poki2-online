What Is Battle Karts?

Battle Karts is a multiplayer kart battle arena — drive around enclosed arenas, pick up weapons, and blast opponents with rockets, mines, and power-ups. It's a top-down kart combat game with real-time online multiplayer.

How to Play

Drive your kart with WASD or arrow keys. Weapons appear as pick-ups scattered around the arena — drive over one to equip it. Use the fire button to launch your weapon at the nearest opponent. Last kart standing wins the round.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys — drive
  • Spacebar — fire weapon

Tips

Rockets home in on the nearest target — use them when you have a clear line of sight. Mines are best dropped near weapon spawn points where opponents are forced to drive. Stay mobile; a stationary kart is an easy target.
