What Is Bombardino Crocodilo Clicker?

Bombardino Crocodilo Clicker is an idle clicker built around the most famous Italian Brainrot meme character — a missile-shaped crocodile. Click Bombardino to earn coins, buy passive upgrades, and unlock increasingly absurd character variants as your empire grows.

How to Play

Click the large Bombardino Crocodilo in the centre of the screen to earn coins. Spend coins on upgrades in the right panel — click power boosters, passive income generators, and milestone unlocks. As passive income grows, coins accumulate automatically even when you stop clicking.

Controls

  • Left Click — click Bombardino to earn coins
  • Mouse — navigate upgrade menu

Tips

Buy passive generators as soon as they become available — the compounding income eclipses manual clicking within minutes. Aim for the milestone unlock at each major coin threshold; they multiply all earnings for the rest of the run.
